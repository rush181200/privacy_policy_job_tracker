Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30th August 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Job Tracker, a Chrome extension designed to help users track job applications by recording details such as the company website, application date and time, company name, and job position, and storing this information in a Google Sheets URL provided by the user.

2. Information We Collect

User-Provided Information: We collect the following information from users:

  • Company website URL
  • Application date and time
  • Company name
  • Job position
  • Additional notes (if provided)

Google Sheets URL: We store the Google Sheets URL provided by the user for the purpose of saving job application details.

Tab URL: We access the URL of the currently active tab to capture the website where the job application is being made.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Data Storage: The information you provide is stored in a Google Sheet at the URL you specify.
  • Application Tracking: The data is used solely for the purpose of tracking job applications and managing the application details you have provided.
  • Authentication: OAuth2 is used to authenticate and authorize access to Google Sheets to save your data securely.

4. Data Access and Security

  • Access Control: Your data is stored in the Google Sheets URL you provide, and access to this data is restricted to you as the user of the extension.
  • Data Security: We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Permissions

  • identity: Used to manage OAuth2 authentication.
  • storage: Used to save user settings such as the Google Sheets URL.
  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the URL of the current tab.
  • scripting: Used to execute scripts necessary for retrieving and storing application data.

6. Data Retention

Your information is retained in the Google Sheet you provide. You can modify or delete this information directly within your Google Sheets.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in this document, and your continued use of the extension will signify your acceptance of the updated terms.
